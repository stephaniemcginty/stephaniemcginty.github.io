Cavalier King Charles Spaniels

The Friendliest Pups

by Stephanie McGinty

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are an extremely friendly and loyal breed. My first dog was a Cavalier Spaniel named Sampson (or Sammy for short). He was of the Blenheim color variety which are reddish-brown and white.
