About Cavaliers

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are an extremely friendly and loyal breed. They are a popular family dog and good with young children and other animals. Small in size, they do well even in apartments. They are also good companions for traveling and outdoor activies such as hiking and camping.

My Dog

My first dog was a Cavalier Spaniel named Sampson (or Sammy for short). He was of the Blenheim color variety which are reddish-brown and white.

I chose Cavalier King Charles Spaniels as my theme since Sammy made a big impact on me as my first dog.

I had always wanted a dog growing up and wasn't allowed to have one until I was fifteen years old. I did a lot of research trying to find the perfect dog and happened to choose Cavaliers. A neighbor of mine happened to own several Cavaliers, so that also helped me make a decision.

Finding a breeder wasn't easy, and the process of locating one and waiting for available puppies took several months. I was very happy to finally bring home my puppy.

Even though Sammy did not end up living a very long life, I am still glad I made the decision to raise a Cavalier. I would like to do so again in the future.
