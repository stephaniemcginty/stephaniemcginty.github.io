About Cavaliers

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are an extremely friendly and loyal breed. They are a popular family dog and good with young children and other animals. Small in size, they do well even in apartments.

My Dog

My first dog was a Cavalier Spaniel named Sampson (or Sammy for short). He was of the Blenheim color variety which are reddish-brown and white.
