Traits

Some personality traits of Cavaliers include:

  • Friendly
  • Loyal
  • Easy going
  • Affectionate
  • Gentle
  • Want to please
  • Can be timid
  • Dependent

Cavaliers are perhaps known best for their very sweet and gentle disposition. They are good around children and other animals. Since they are eager to please, they can be trained without too much difficulty. They are also very loyal to their owners and bond to their family.

Separation Anxiety

Cavaliers make great family pets, but can sometimes become too dependant on their owners.

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are known for being very dependent on their owners. They love to follow you around the house and want to be where you are. Unfortunately, this means that they suffer from separation anxiety when left alone. If you intend to become a Cavalier owner, it is best if you have a schedule that allows you to spend a lot of time with your dog.

Crate training is especially difficult with this breed due to their severe separation anxiety. Proper training from an early age can help mediate this anxiety.
