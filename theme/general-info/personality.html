Traits

Some personality traits of Cavaliers include:

  • Friendly
  • Loyal
  • Easy going
  • Affectionate
  • Gentle
  • Want to please
  • Can be timid
  • Dependent
Cavaliers make great family pets, but can sometimes become too dependant on their owners.

Separation Anxiety

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are known for being very dependent on their owners. They love to follow you around the house and want to be where you are. Unfortunately, this means that they suffer from separation anxiety when left alone. If you intend on becoming a Cavalier owner, it is best if you have a schedule that allows you to spend a lot of time with the dog.

Crate training is especially difficult with this breed due to their severe separation anxiety. Proper training from an early age can help mediate this anxiety.
