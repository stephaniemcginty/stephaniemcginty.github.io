Traits

Some personality traits of Cavaliers include:

  • Friendly
  • Loyal
  • Easy going
  • Affectionate
  • Gentle
  • Want to please
  • Can be timid
  • Dependent

Cavaliers make great family pets, but can sometimes become too dependant.

Separation Anxiety

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are known for being very dependent on their owners. They love to follow you around the house and want to be where you are. Unfortunately, this means that they suffer from separation anxiety when left alone. Proper training can mediate this anxiety.
