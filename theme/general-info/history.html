by Stephanie McGinty

Origin

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels originated in the United Kingdom and paintings of the little dog date back as far as the 16th century. King Charles II was very fond of the breed, hence the name.

The standard for the breed wasn't developed until the 1920's.

More Info

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels were used as lap dogs for royalty.
