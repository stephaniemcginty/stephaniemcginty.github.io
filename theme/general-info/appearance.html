Color Varieties

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels can come in four distinct color varieties:

  • Blenheim (brown/red and white)
  • Ruby (red)
  • Tricolor (black, brown/red, and white)
  • Black and Tan

Blenheim

Perhaps the most popular color is Blenheim. The name Blenheim comes from John Churchill, 1st Duke of Marlborough's estate named "Blenheim". The duke owned several Cavalier type spaniels.

Size

The Cavalier King Charles Spaniel is a small dog and considered a "Toy" breed. They weigh about 13-18 pounds, although my Cavalier weighed around 20 pounds. They are about 12-13 inches tall.
