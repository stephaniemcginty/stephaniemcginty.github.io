Common Health Concerns

Some common health concerns of Cavalier King Charles Spaniels include:

  • Heart problems (mitral valve disease or MVD)
  • Neurological problems (syringomyelia)
  • Potential for obesity
  • Hip dysplasia
  • Eye problems

Average Life Span

Unfortunately, Cavaliers are not the healthiest of dog breeds which has the potential for expensive vet bills.

Their life span can be anywhere from 9 to 14 years. However, my Cavalier only lived to be 8 years old, probably suffering from heart issues.
