Common Health Concerns

Some common health concerns of Cavalier King Charles Spaniels include:

  • Heart problems (mitral valve disease or MVD)
  • Neurological problems (syringomyelia)
  • Potential for obesity
  • Hip dysplasia
  • Eye problems
  • Epilepsy
  • Deafness

Average Life Span

Unfortunately, Cavaliers are not the healthiest of dog breeds which has the potential for expensive vet bills. This is due to the fact that all Cavaliers today descend from six dogs, so inherited disease is extremely common. The most common cause of death in Cavaliers is heart failure due to mitral valve disease.

Their life span can be anywhere from 9 to 14 years. However, my Cavalier only lived to be 8 years old, probably suffering from heart issues and/or a stroke.
